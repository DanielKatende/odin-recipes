Danie's Mouth Watering Pizza

Absolutely Homemade Pizza!What makes this recipe even better is that its easy enough for beginners, and most of the work is hands-off. The pizza dough is versatile, so you can use it to make garlic brown bread and Italian bread. It can also be frozen for future use.

Do try this out, and be prepared to be flooded with pizza requests!!

Ingredients for the recipe

If you use 250ml cup, these measurements will be enough for 4 personal pizzas.

  • 2 cups all purpose flour (maida)
  • 1.5 cups whole wheat flour (atta)
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tbsp curd
  • 4 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 + 1/4 tsp instant dry yeast

For Making one pizza:

  • 1/4 of the pizza dough
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Store bought pizza sauce
  • Grated mozzarella cheese
  • Topping of choice - onions/tomatoes/capsium/black olives,sausages

Steps for making the Pizza

  1. Take 1 cup warm water
  2. Mix sugar and yeast in the water. Allow to rest for 10 mins until frothy. Do not add salt in this water.
  3. In a mixing bowl take the remaining dough ingredients, maida, atta, olive oil, curd and salt.
  4. Add the activated yeast solution and knead well for about 10 mins till the dough iss soft and pliable.
  5. Cover the mixing bowl with the dough with a cling film and allow it to rest in a warm place for about 1 hr until the dough rises and doubles in size.
  6. After an hour punch it lightly to releasse the air, and knead gently for a minute.
  7. Take one fourth of the dough and roll it out into a circle on a flat surface. Fold the edges so that they rise above the center. Brush the surface with olivw oil. Allow the rolled out dough to rest for 15 mins.
  8. Spread a generous amount of pizza sauce, and sprinkle the cheese.
  9. Take toppings of choice and Spread them evenly over the sprinkled cheese.
  10. Now bake the pizza in a pre-heated oven at 250 degrees C/480 degrees F for about 20 minutes until the cheese has melted and the edges look golden brown.
  11. Take out the baked pizza and slice it.
  12. Enjoy the pizza with some seasoning and mustard sauce !!
