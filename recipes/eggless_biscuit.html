Whole wheat, Eggless biscuits Recipe

These whole wheat, egglesss biscuits use stample pantry ingredients, and are super easy to make. Even my 5 year old loves to help me when I make them.

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp ghee
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar / jaggery powder / unrefined sugar
  • 1 cup whole wheat flour (atta)
  • 1/3 tsp baking powder
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder (optional)
  • 4-5 tbsp milk

Steps needed for recipe

  1. Sift in atta, baking powder, a pinch of salt and cardamom powder
  2. Combine roughly and add milk
  3. Combine lightly with hands till you get a soft crumbly dough
  4. Add a tablespoon or two milkif the dough is falling apart
  5. Shape the dough into a log and wrap in cling wrap
  6. Refrigerate for 30-40 minutes
  7. Cut 1/4 inch thick slices slices from the chilled log and place on a baking tray lined with parchment
  8. Bake at 170 degrees C/340 degrees F for 15-17 minutes till golden brown
  9. Once done, take out and cool on a wire rack
  10. Your eggless, whole wheat biscuits are ready. Enjoy!!
